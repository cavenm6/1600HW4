## Timeline of the Battle

### **Day 1 — July 1, 1863**
- Confederate forces pushed Union troops through Gettysburg  
- Union forces formed defensive positions on Cemetery Hill

### **Day 2 — July 2**
- Fighting at iconic locations:
  - Little Round Top  
  - Devil’s Den  
  - The Wheatfield  
- Union successfully held high ground

### **Day 3 — July 3 (Pickett’s Charge)**
> “An attack so grand in scale, yet doomed from the start.”

12,500 Confederate soldiers advanced across open fields toward Cemetery Ridge.  
The charge failed — marking the turning point of the Civil War.

---

##  Why Gettysburg Mattered

- Ended Lee’s hope of invading the North  
- Boosted Union morale  
- Led to Lincoln’s **Gettysburg Address**
